# DECA Euro Windows & Doors

## Overview
European tilt-and-turn windows and doors, **manufactured in the USA** (Westfield, MA).

## Key Facts
- **Factory-direct** — no distributors, no markup
- **Turnaround**: ~4 weeks from order
- **Ships nationwide**
- **Profile systems**: [[03_Products/GEALAN-LINEAR|GEALAN LINEAR]], [[03_Products/GEALAN-S8000-IQ|GEALAN S8000 IQ]]
- **Glazing**: Triple glazing available
- **R-Value**: Up to 7.7
- **Security**: Up to 11 locking points
- **Warranty**: 15 years frame / 10 years glass

## Value Proposition
The quality and performance of European windows, with American manufacturing speed and support. No import delays, no container shipping risks.
