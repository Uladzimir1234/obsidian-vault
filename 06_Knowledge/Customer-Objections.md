# Customer Objections

Based on analysis of **958 sales calls**.

## Common Objections

1. **Price shock** — European windows cost more than big-box alternatives
2. **Brand trust** — "Never heard of DECA" / unfamiliar brand
3. **Tilt-turn confusion** — Don't understand the mechanism or benefits
4. **Installation fear** — Worried about finding qualified installers
5. **No financing** — Sticker shock without payment plan options
6. **Long silence = lost** — If customer goes quiet for 2+ weeks, deal is likely dead

## Handling Tips
- Price: emphasize factory-direct (no middleman), R-value 7.7, 15yr warranty
- Brand: show factory, explain European engineering made in USA
- Tilt-turn: video demos work best
- Installation: offer installer referral network
- Silence: follow up at day 3, 7, 14 — then move to nurture
